About Enabler

Enabler is an accessibility platform built to bridge the gap between spoken language and sign language. We believe everyone deserves equal access to communication — at work, at school, and in everyday life.

Our AI-powered tools help deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals participate fully in conversations, while giving hearing users the ability to communicate naturally in sign language. Enabler is designed with accessibility at its core: high-contrast interfaces, keyboard navigation, and screen reader support.

  • Built for real-world accessibility needs
  • Supports multiple sign languages
  • Privacy-first design
  • Works across mobile, tablet, and desktop
